Free music!

Fri 03 August 2007
By rgommers

In Life.

Well first I'll say hi to everyone, since this is my first post and I'm happy to share Jing's blog. Now on topic: do you want some great free music? Then go here. Magnatune is a great music site, I've bought several albums from them already. Now they've decided to give an album away for free each month, so try it and download if you like it! Also, if you buy anything you can legally give three more copies away to your friends. So if you read this leave a comment and you're next on the list for that:)
