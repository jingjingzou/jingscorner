Triathlon

Sat 26 October 2013
By zouzoujing

In Sport.

Triathlon is a multi-stage competition. One of its most popular and well-known forms is swimming, cycling and running. One of my new year's resolutions for 2013 is to run a mini-triathlon. Mini can be 1/8 of an 'iron man' for example, which is 500m swimming, followed by 22.5km cycling and end with a 5km run. I am not a great planner and I only started looking for the triathlon competition when summer starts. Consequently, the one in Nuenen, which is pretty much the only one close by, was already full. However, in the end, I still managed to get myself registered by contacting the organizer. But it did not come free since I needed to pay 10 EURO extra and I could only attend the trio teams, meaning I will have to fill my name 4 times as the team capitan and members of my own team. Not surprisingly, I named my team 'hahaha'. I just need to teach myself a lesson that I have to plan better in order to get things done in a normal way.

All in all, I made it with an OK time, see photos.

But I do have to mention again that due to no preparation, it took me triple amount of time for changing. Nevertheless, it has been great experience. Lesson learnt, I will have a much better event next year for the 1/4.
